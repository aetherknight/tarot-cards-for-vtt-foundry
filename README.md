# tarot-cards-for-vtt-foundry

These tarot card images, based on the Rider Waite Smith Tarot deck, have been
edited to make the cards' numerical and suits more legible for online play of
games like [His Majesty the Worm](https://www.hismajestytheworm.games/).

This [Foundry VTT](https://foundryvtt.com/) module provides several decks of
cards that use the Tarot Card for VTT card images, with a focus on improving
the readability of the cards' suits and numerical values for tarot-based games
like His Majesty the Worm. The card images prioritize consistency and
readability of the suit and numbers, but still retain much of the original
Rider-Waite-Smith cards' art.

## Included Card Decks

The module adds a compendium pack that includes several decks:

- **Separate player and GM decks for use with His Majesty the Worm**. The player
  deck contains the minor arcana and the Fool, while the GM deck contains the
  other major arcana. Note that if you intend to play His Majesty the Worm
  using Foundry, you should take a look at other modules that help improve the
  workflows of drawing cards for Tests of Fate or during the Challenge Phase.

- **Standard Rider-Waite-Smith Tarot decks**. One deck uses arabic numeral
  cards, while the other deck uses roman numerals for most of the cards, as
  well as letters for aces and face cards.
